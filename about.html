About Me


I spent ten years in retail technology support before deciding to make the switch to software development. The ten years I spent in support helped me hone my troubleshooting and problem-solving skills that I now use to debug software. I've always had a passion for technology, and I love building things.

When I'm not working, I love to travel with my wife, explore nature, watch bad sci-fi movies, and play video games.

If you're interested in working together on a project, or you'd like some recommendations on travel spots contact me.
